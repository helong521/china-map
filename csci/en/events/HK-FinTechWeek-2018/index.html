will attend The World's First Cross-border FinTech Event

Oct 29 - Nov 2, 2018

Hong Kong - Shenzhen

C06 Site, Hong Kong, Convention & Exhibition Centre, 1 Expo Drive, Wan Chai

HONG KONG FINTECH WEEK

香港金融科技周

The world's first cross-border financial technology event, the Financial Technology Week, will be held in Hong Kong and Shenzhen by the Hong Kong Monetary Authority and the Invest Hong Kong. The event will attract more than 8,000 senior executives, and more than 200 of the world's top financial technology founders, investors, regulators and academics to speak at the conference.

China Securities Credit Investment was invited to participate in the exhibition, and hopes to discuss new trends in the financial technology industry with the community on this platform and shape the future of financial services.
