Perfect Training System

We have prepared the comprehensive talents training plan for our employees. The company's four talents training plan, that is, seek dream, chase dream, build dream and realize dream, is the specialized program that is developed aiming at employees' ability and professionalism needs to be developed in their each stage from the interns to the workplace elites.

Achieve A Better Future

Broad Room For Growth

We provide employees with the diversified internal and external communication opportunities. Employees can participate in different challenging projects, develop their merits, and continuously improve their professional competence and professionalism in actual fights, so as to achieve in-depth development in the professional fields, or cross-field horizontal career development. We respect each employee's self-development appeals and escort for their development.

Training System

  • Seek Dream

    The seeking dream plan is an intern-training program. Each intern has his/her an internship tutor to help develop a special internship program and provide training and guidance in professional skills and career development.

  • Chase Dream

    The chasing dream plan is a training program for new graduates. It aims to help newcomers in workplace to quickly integrate into the team, master business knowledge and work skills, and achieve better change from a student to a worker.

  • Build Dream

    The building dream plan is a program of training business backbone for the company. It aims to help cadre employees in the business segments of the Group strengthen business communication and collaborative development, and enhance team management capability so as to quickly promote team performance.

  • Realize Dream

    The realizing dream plan is a program of training middle and senior executives for the company. It is designed to help managers expand their frontier vision, improve organizational management ability and the ability of mastering the whole situation.

"Plus+ Energy" Employee Training Series is an interactive learning platform for overall employees of China Securities Credit Investment Group, covering: industry trends, business knowledge, product introduction, workplace skills and professionalism training. The purpose is to provide a wealth of training resources for employees’ diversified career development, and employees can choose appropriate training freely so as to achieve the accumulation of more positive energy for individuals and the company.
