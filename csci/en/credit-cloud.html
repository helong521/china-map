Service Scene

The Risk Monitoring of Targent Investments

Financial institutions need to quickly identify potential risks to project participants, proactively make judgment and address, reduce or even avoid them.We provide data solutions for all participants of the client's target investments (such as ABS, private placement bonds, and etc.), including stakeholders, administrators, service trusts and rating guarantors. To identify the co-related risk, we add in the enterprise's shareholders to watch, monitor their public sentiment data from the past half-year, which include news and public opinions ( from WeChat, Weibo, Baidu Tieba , forums, and various social media), and push the incremental data daily to the clients. By leveraging the advanced NLP technology and expert experience gained in the capital markets, we conduct effective correlation filtration of massive public sentiments and quickly analyze their positive and negative impacts. As a result, the client gets the risk early warnings that may have huge potential effect on the operation of the enterprise on watch.

Supply Chain Finance Platform Services

A fintech platform, which is built to service all enterprises through supply chain, leverages distributed ledger technology to keep data secure, authentic, and traceable. By integrating with different parties to synchronize data flow with business flow, users could initiate and pass down digitalized account receivable and also get financed of it in an efficient manner.

Service Features

  • Activating Existing AR

    Activate account receivables of different tiers of suppliers in anchor company’s supply chain by providing finance services to them based on anchor’s credit capability instead of suppliers’ own credit capability. This will mitigate the liquidity pressure of suppliers and reduce the overall risk of the supply chain.

  • Improving Efficiency

    Instead of connect with all parties through the supply chain, funders could just connect with one blockchain node to get all synchronized information with authorization through distributed network. This will significantly reduce the cost of integration and improve efficiency.

  • Managing Data Authorization

    All the data of transactions are encrypted before being stored in distributed ledger. Only authorized users get access to relevant data. Authorization management can be applied to every field to build a complicated decentralized system.

  • Mitigating Risk

    The distributed ledger based architecture ensures the safety, authenticity and traceability of data. Smart contract technology could minimize the mistake of excessive manual operation and fraud in data input and verification process.

Service Scene

This supply chain finance platform helps financial institutions to provide financial services to whole chain enterprises based on anchor company’s credit capability. By activating the liquidation of account receivables from various tier suppliers, the operation risk of suppliers is reduced. Moreover, closed loop of supply chain network will reduce the financial risk through the process.

Financial services include invoice financing, account receivable transfer and etc. On the one hand, for different tier suppliers, the accounts receivable verified by anchor company can be directly used to pay for the goods from upstream suppliers to reduce the suppliers’ demand for liquidity; on the other hand, the suppliers of various tiers that hold accounts receivable could apply for financing via the platform. Supply chain finance platform will match funders for the suppliers to expand the suppliers’ access to get financing, reduce their financing cost and significantly reduce their liquidity risk.

Distributed Ledger Services

We offer a leading financial service platform based on Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT), focus on the innovation of DLT product and empower our business partners to quickly build a scalable business applications.

Service Features

  • Personal privacy protection

    All on-chain data are privacy-protected by using asymmetic cryptographic techniques and trasmitted in a P2P manner. Hacker cannnot interpret the cipher text without private key decription.

  • No caching of data

    All on-chain data are transmitted in a P2P manner, DLT platform does not cache any intermediate results.

  • Support to financial asset business process

    A wide varieties of financial assets, including individual customer assets, enterprise assets, ABS, non-performing assets, and credit enhancement assets can be uploaded to the DLT platform, to realize DLT-enabled business flow covering the asset entire life cycle.

Service Function

  • API access to blockchain

    Data can be easily uploaded to the DLT platform. Independent development of an blockchain application requires great technical competence and high research & development costs. API access enables building the digital assets of third party institutions, trusted user data and application scenes on the blockchain, forming an universal asset management platform to greatly save time and labor costs.

  • Multi-level permission control

    With private access to control encrypted data, stakeholders have the permission to view data, and others have to be authorized before querying the data.

  • Support to financial transaction

    Financial distributed ledger architecture is adopted for transaction processing that the financial scene cares most, such as transaction sequence and transaction atomicity support.

  • Business Intelligence backed by DLT

    Provide data analytic tools and interfaces for on-chain data that supports business flow.

Service Scene

Financial credit
