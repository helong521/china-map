Credit Rating Services

From the perspective of internal & external ratings, objectively and timely evaluate the credit risks of bond issuers and counter parties

  • Internal Credit Rating
  • External Credit Rating
Overview

In order to provide intelligent credit warning, China Securities Credit Investment designed an enterprise credit risk assessment and monitoring system. The system performs timely assessments on the credit risk of the bond issuer and the counterparty, utilizing big data, machine learning and other technologies. Through the construction of investment target pool, current holding pool, and high-risk bond pool, which dynamically changes as data changes, the system covers risk management needs before, during and after investment and help our clients improve risk management capabilities.

Service advantages

  • Integrated services

    Base on the understanding of market demand, provide integrated bond market credit risk management schemes covering “dynamic risk database, intelligent assessment model, professional risk management system and professional analyst comment”, and support investors in rapidly building a credit risk management system suiting themselves.

  • Full coverage

    Quantified credit risk assessment model system covering the whole market, comprehensive bond market risk analysis data and nearly 400 risk model indexes.

  • Intelligent and real-time approach

    With big data, conduct real-time and dynamic monitoring, provide early warning, intelligent early warning information analysis, and detect and push message in a real time manner.

Application scenario

Risk management

Join hands with financial institutions in establishing an internal unified credit assessment standard, timely assess the credit risks of bond issuers and counter parties, dynamically monitor concentration risk, and support many scenes of risk management authority, such as credit business review and post-investment dynamic management.

Investment with fixed income

Dynamically assess the credit risks of bond issuers and counter parties to fully cover the bond market, establish a dynamic access pool, a bond holding pool, an investment prohibition pool; dynamically monitor the change in the credit-worthiness of bond issuers, cover many scenes of businesses with fixed income, including pre-investment access, decision-making during investment and post-investment dynamic management.

Investment research

Make use of structured data on enterprise finance and operation, and market public opinion information, support investment researchers in research on investment strategies and preparation of investment research reports.

Overview

This services are provided by CSCI Pengyuan and its Hong Kong licensed subsidiary, Pengyuan International. CSCI Pengyuan is a majority-owned subsidiary of China Securities Credit Investment.

In accordance with industry norms and rating methodologies, China Securities Credit Investment performs solicited, unsolicited and monitoring ratings on a variety of subjects. It includes corporate credit, corporate bonds, asset-backed securities, collective trust funds, financial institutions, corporate governance, and overseas dollar bonds. The ratings also abide by the principle of independence, objectivity and fairness.

Service advantages

  • Extensive experience in rating

    The company has devoted itself to the rating industry for 25 years, with extensive experience in rating, and has established a complete rating methods and standard system.

  • Qualification for overseas rating

    Pengyuan International, a subsidiary of Pengyuan Credit, has obtained a license for Type 10: Providing Credit Rating Services as issued by SFC for overseas rating activity.

  • Services covering the country

    The Company has established 9 total branches in Beijing, Shanghai, Hunan, Jiangsu, Sichuan, Shandong, Jilin, Shaanxi and Zhengzhou, and can serve the whole country.

Subject and bond rating
