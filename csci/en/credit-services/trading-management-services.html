Overview

The company has strategically planned to provide professional asset valuation, pricing and credit analysis services to fixed-income investors. Overview

The company provides traders with fixed income securities trading information, including real-time quoted price of bonds and transactions,interbank financing and deposit. The information is displayed through Dealing Matrix (DM), a one-stop pricing information platform of the inter-institutional market.

This service is providered by Inno Dealing Information, a strategic equity investment company of China Securities Credit Investment. Inno Dealing Information possesses multitue of financial technology software patents, as well as a highly experienced customer service team. Currently，more than 18,000 traders from 4,500 financial institutions accross the country are using Inno Dealing Technology's productto identify market opportunities.
