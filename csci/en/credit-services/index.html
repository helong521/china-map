Data Services

Provide risk data based on scenarios, support decision-making in credit risk management

Overview

Focusing on credit risk management, China Securities Credit Investment has built a core credit database for the capital markets. The database is designed to support comprehensive, timely and accurate intelligent credit data services.,facilitating our clients with opportunity mining, pre-investment due diligence, investment analysis, and post-investment monitoring.

The database covers about 55,000 core enterprises in the capital markets (including listed companies on Shanghai and Shenzhen Stock Exchange, bond issuing companies, NEEQ listed companies, and financial institutions etc.), 30 million corporate enterprises and 80 million sole proprietorship businesses. The data is legally collected from compliant data sources and covers information from multiple dimensions, such as basic commercial information, legal disposition, financial statement, operational data, and public news etc.

Service advantages

  • Perspective of credit risk

    Display enterprise’s industrial and commercial information, shareholder information, financial information, operation information, litigation information and listing announcements, integrate various information from the perspective of credit risk assessment for providing comprehensive assessment material.

  • Massive compliance data

    Possess compliant and legal data sources from traceable sources and form a stable and reliable data resource pool.

  • In-depth application of AI

    Use natural semantic processing technology, create credit risk knowledge graph, explore the connection and beneficial relationship between enterprises, monitor public opinion and negative information, and provide early warning against enterprise risk conduction.

Application scenario

Credit risk management

Establish a subject credit risk monitoring system, use enterprise connection graphs, and provide rapid, comprehensive and intelligent early warning against credit risk.

Seeking business opportunities

Use industrial and hot spot data dynamically extracted and enterprise connections, and search for business opportunities in an in-depth and systematic manner.
