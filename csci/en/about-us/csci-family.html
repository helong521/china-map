China Securities Credit Service Co., Ltd. (“CSCS ”), was established on Sepember 28th, 2015 and is registered in Qianhai, Shenzhen. It has a registered capital of RMB 142.8 million and is majority-owned by China Securities Credit Investment.

As an innovative credit risk management service provider, CSCS serves as the data technology foundation of the company's credit value chain. It focuses on the construction of credit technology infrastructure in the financial market and is dedicated to providing intelligent credit data, credit assessment, data-driven risk control and early warning services for the regulators, enterprises, investment institutions and other financial participants.

China Securities Credit Technology Co., Ltd. (“CSCT”) was jointly established by China Securities Credit Investment and eastmoney.com on December 16th, 2016 and is registered in Futian District, Shenzhen. It has a registered capital of RMB 100 million.

Aspiring to reshape the value of consumer finance credit, CSCT exports four core competencies: data-driven risk control, settlement and clearing, operation monitoring and project investment for the consumer finance industry. It provides guarantors and funders with filterable, penetrating, real-time monitoring asset service solutions. It also provides asset service provider with low-cost, scalable and flexible financial solutions.

CSCI Pengyuan Credit Rating Limited (“CSCI Pengyuan”), established in 1993, is one of the oldest rating agencies in China. In 2016, China Securities Credit Investment strategically invested in CSCI Pengyuan, and this business has become an important part of the Group's credit value chain. CSCI Pengyuan is committed to providing investors with independent, objective and fair corporate credit rating, bond and structured financing product rating and corporate governance rating services.

Pengyuan Credit Rating (Hong Kong) Co., Ltd. (“Pengyuan International”) was established in 2011 in Hong Kong, China. With accurate understanding of China's credit market, Pengyuan International relies on the Group's database and advanced information processing capabilities to provide investors with timely and credible rating services in the international market.

Shanghai Inno Dealing Information Technology Ltd. (“Inno Dealing Information”) is a fintech company invested by China Securities Credit Investment focusing on inter-institutional trading quotation services. Currently，more than 18,000 traders from 4,500 financial institutions accross the country are using Inno Dealing Technology's productto identify market opportunities. Adhering to the concept of openness, sharing and win-win, the company has built a transparent, professional and open quotation information exchange platform for various types of financial institutions based on the in-depth exploration of big data in the financial market.

CSCI Capital Management Co., Ltd (“CSCI Capital”) was established on Sepember 16th, 2015 and is registered in Qianhai, Shenzhen. It has a registered capital of RMB 100 million and is a wholly-owned subsidiary of China Securities Credit Investment.It has been issued with a certificate of manager of privately-raised funds by the Asset Management Association of China.

Empowered by Group's data -driven risk control ability and credit technology, China Securities Credit Capital aims to develop into a light-asset platform, continuously improve its competence in assets screening and resources integration. CSCI Capital aims to obtain stable investment returns and build an excllent investment advisory and asset management brand.

CSCI Asset Management Co., Ltd (“CSCI Asset Management”) was established on October 15th, 2015 and is registered in Pudong District, Shanghai. It has a registered capital of RMB 100 million and is a wholly-owned subsidiary of China Securities Credit Investment.

CSCI Asset Management develops and exports three competences, i.e., acquisition of assets, due diligence and valuation, disposal and restructuring.The company leverages asset management methods to restructure and dispose resources in order to resolve the risk of non-performing assets. It also makes use of investment, debt restructuring, asset restructuring, and debt-to-equity swaps to dispose, restructure and optimize special assets that cannot generate normal cash flow.
